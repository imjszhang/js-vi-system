# Cyber-Taoist Character Specification

## Overall Style

**Hard Environment (Neo-Brutalist) + Soft Character (Organic Curves) = Conflict and Reconciliation**

## Physical Specifications

### Proportions
- **Head-to-body ratio**: 7.5–8 heads tall
- **Build**: Slender and upright — NOT chibi/cute, projects mature sage presence
- **Silhouette**: Flowing organic curves (流动的有机曲线), no sharp geometric angles on the body

### Hair
- **Style**: Spiky upright hair (硬刺形竖立短发)
- **Character**: Sharp, angular, aggressive
- **Purpose**: Creates contrast against the soft flowing body — Cyberpunk edge

### Face / Mask
- **JS Logo as half-mask**: Covers the eye region and above, exposing the jawline and mouth contour
- **Mask details**:
  - Yellow circle `#FCD228` background
  - Black "JS" letters centered
  - `3px` black stroke
  - `4px` hard shadow
- **Exposed area**: Lower face only — jawline and mouth visible

### Chest Energy Core
- **Position**: Center of chest
- **Size**: Approximately fist-sized
- **Appearance**: Glowing yellow `#FCD228` circular energy core
- **Radiating patterns**: Circuit/meridian textures extending outward
  - Primary lines: Black (`#000000`)
  - Glowing branches: Yellow (`#FCD228`) — spread across the entire body
- **Animation**: Subtle pulsing — symbolizes the life force and technical energy of the "Channel" (信道)

## Pose: Growth Hacker Advance Stance

- **Body**: Leaning slightly forward (~15 degrees)
- **Left hand**: Naturally in pocket — casual confidence
- **Right hand**: Raised, operating a floating virtual interface — tech command
- **Expression**: Alert, decisive — stillness with edge (静中带锐)

## Core Visual Tensions

| Element | Source | Character |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| Spiky hair | Cyberpunk | Hard, aggressive |
| Flowing body | Taoist | Soft, organic |
| Advance stance | Growth Hacker | Proactive, decisive |
| Logo mask | Brand identity | Mysterious, iconic |
| Energy core | 信道 (Channel) | Alive, pulsing |

## Usage Guidelines

| Context | Variant |
|---------|---------|
| Hero section / landing page | Full body, with floating UI elements |
| Profile avatar / social media | Head + shoulders (bust), mask prominent |
| Inline icon / small spaces | JS Logo mask only (circle) |
| Blog post / article header | Upper body, advance pose |
