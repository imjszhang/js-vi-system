# Brand Identity

**JS** is a Cyberpunk Growth Hacker who walks the **Dao** (信道).

## Four Dimensions

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Cultural Root** | Chinese, follows the Chinese concept of "Dao" — acting in accordance with momentum (顺势而为), governing through non-action (无为而治) — systems wisdom |
| **Spiritual Character** | Cyberpunk — technological libertarianism, hacker spirit dwelling on the boundary between digital and physical |
| **Methodology** | Growth Hacker — rapid experimentation, data validation, viral growth |
| **Professional Identity** | Cross-domain AI application explorer — from medical research to Agent ecosystems, seeking the universal underlying logic of technology |

## Full-Stack Capabilities

- **Technology**: Programming, AI/Agent development, product architecture
- **Business**: Internet operations, marketing, financial investment
- **Research**: AI applications in specific scenarios, systems-level logic
- **Strategy**: Cross-platform ecosystem analysis, long-term system building

## Original Insights

1. **AI's ceiling is its user** — AI has no survival pressure, no death, thus a ceiling on evolution; human survival anxiety is the true driver of evolution
2. **The internet has surface/inner worlds, migration is underway** — the surface world (websites/apps) degrades to a database, the inner world (personal Agents) becomes the new interaction layer
3. **Don't build spectacles, build systems** — tech spectacles are liabilities not assets; in Civilization, beginners chase wonders, masters build compound systems
